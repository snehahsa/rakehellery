How the Game
Works

Rakehellery is a 6-player on-chain game built into CA. Entry is $30 worth of tokens per seat. You pick an empty seat by sending tokens to that seat address (burn addresses 0x000…0001–0x000…0006). Your entry is burned as the stake.

When all six seats are filled ($180 pot), the game waits. The next buy/sell swap worth at least 2× the entry ($60) settles it: 1st gets $100 worth of tokens, 2nd gets $30, and $50 stays burned. No admin payout.

Four steps

I

Pick a seat

Send any tiny amount of the token to seat 0x…0001–0x…0006. One empty seat per wallet per game.

II

Entry burns

Entry fee is $30 worth of tokens. The contract burns that full amount from your balance. The first player locks the token price for the whole table.

III

Wait for a trade

After six seats fill, winners are not drawn yet. A Uniswap buy or sell ≥ $60 (2× entry) settles the game.

IV

Winners paid

1st is minted $100 worth of tokens, 2nd $30. The last $50 stays burned forever.
