How the Game
Works

Rakehellery is a 6-player on-chain game built into CA. Entry is $30 worth of tokens per seat. You pick an empty seat by sending tokens to that seat address. Your entry is burnt as the stake.

  • Seat #1→0x000…0001
  • Seat #2→0x000…0002
  • Seat #3→0x000…0003
  • Seat #4→0x000…0004
  • Seat #5→0x000…0005
  • Seat #6→0x000…0006

When all six seats are filled ($180 pool), the game waits. The next buy/sell settles it: 1st gets $100 worth of tokens, 2nd gets a full $30 refund and $50 worth burns forever. Fully random. Fully degen.

Four steps

I

Pick a seat

Send any tiny amount of the token to an open seat address eg: Seat #3 0x000…0003. Must check if Open but if not, No tokens are burnt

II

Entry burns

Entry fee is $30 worth of tokens. The contract burns that specific amount from your balance automatically when you pick a seat.

III

Wait for a trade

After all 6 seats fill, A buy or sell settles the winners on-chain, built into the CA. Fully Fair and Random.

IV

Winners paid

1st Winner gets $100 Worth Of Tokens, 2nd gets $30 Fully Refunded. The other pool of $50 is burnt forever.
